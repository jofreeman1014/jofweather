Cloudiness vs. Latitude

Through my analysis, cloudiness doesn't seem to have any relation to latitude. According to the data, there aren't any notable trends across my latitude range. That being said, the readings for cloudiness according to openweathermap seem to be binned across a certain range. Notice the distinct rows that the data points all line up on across the plot. Further study could be conducted with this idea using a different weather source in order to track any underlying trends, though this preliminary data suggests that there latitude has little to no effect on cloudiness readings on average.

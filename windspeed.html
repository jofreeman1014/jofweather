Wind Speeds vs. Latitude

Wind speeds appear to have some trend across latitudes. While there are some points across our range that tend towards low wind speeds, peak wind speed appear to grow as the distance from the equator increases. While it's not a continuous trend, this observation is worth noting as it's present in the data acquired. Further analysis on this data could include a study on how elevation or distance from oceans, seas, or other bodies of water have an effect on wind speeds.

Welcome - Longitude, Latitude, Attitude

Welcome to my study on weather patterns across a variety of latitudes. The question is fairly fundamental: how does weather change as you approach the equator? While a simple question to tackle, proving it through data is where the fun begins! In this page, I analyze weather statistics across randomly selected weather stations, with a large pool of data spanning various latitudes. Click any of the images to view a plot using data pulled from openweathermap.
